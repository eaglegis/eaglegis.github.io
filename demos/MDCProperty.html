E-Rates Service

Our new E-rates service means your invoice won't get lost in the letterbox. Now we can email your bill and for extra peace of mind we can even send early reminders before your rates are due.

 Why have we introduced E-Rates? It's just another way to pay your rates. You can also set up a direct-debit, come into the Council offices in person or send us a cheque in the mail.

What are the benefits of E-Rates? Less paper in your letterbox, you can always find the invoice and you may find it more convenient. They choice is yours.
